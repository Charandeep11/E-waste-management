About Us

The E-waste Facility Locator is a platform dedicated to promoting responsible e-waste disposal and recycling.Our mission is to provide individuals with easy access to information about nearby e-waste collection and recycling facilities,educate them about the environmental and health impacts of improper e-waste disposal and incentivize responsible behaviour through a rewards system

Objective:Design and develop an online website that allows users to to easily locate e_waste recycling facilities based on their location.

Target Audience: Individuals looking to recycle electronic items,businesses with bulk e-waste,environmental organizations and government bodies

Thank you for joining us in our mission to create a sustainable future for all
